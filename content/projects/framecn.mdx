## Overview

Free, open-source video components for React built on Editframe with shadcn/ui-compatible registry workflows.

## The problem

Programmatic video generation often requires bespoke composition APIs and separate render pipelines.

## Wins

Composable video layers, timeline control, and cloud MP4 export through Editframe with familiar shadcn-style DX.
