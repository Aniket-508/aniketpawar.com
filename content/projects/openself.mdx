## Overview

Openself helps you build professional resumes through a conversational AI interface. Describe your experience in natural language and watch structured resume sections appear in a live preview.

The app keeps parsing on the server while file data stays in the browser, with full TypeScript and Zod validation across resume schemas.

## The problem

Traditional resume builders force rigid forms and generic templates. Updating a resume for different roles means duplicating work across tools, and most products treat AI as a one-shot generator rather than a collaborative editor.

I wanted something that feels like pair-programming for your career story — iterative, structured, and export-ready.

## Wins

Shipped an end-to-end flow from chat → structured resume → PDF download with interactable sections the AI can update in place.

Integrated Tambo AI for component-level control so the model can edit header, experience, projects, and skills as first-class UI blocks.
