## Overview

Free, open-source Open Graph image components for React built on Satori with live DOM previews.

## The problem

Social preview images are often one-off JSX templates that are hard to reuse across products.

## Wins

Copy-paste OG components with scaled live previews, Satori rendering, and shadcn/ui registry compatibility.
