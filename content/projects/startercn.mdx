## Overview

A template for building and publishing custom shadcn registry components with docs, landing page, and deploy-ready registry output.

## The problem

Publishing a shadcn-compatible registry usually means wiring up docs, registry build, and deployment from scratch.

## Wins

Fork-ready template with Fumadocs, registry build scripts, and `npx shadcn add` compatibility out of the box.
