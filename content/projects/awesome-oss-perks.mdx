## Overview

Awesome OSS Perks aggregates free tiers, credits, and sponsor programs so maintainers can find benefits without hunting across vendor blogs.

Contributions follow the awesome-list format for easy community updates.

## The problem

Perks for OSS are scattered across documentation, tweets, and expired landing pages. New maintainers often miss infrastructure credits that could extend project runway.

## Wins

Centralized, categorized index with clear eligibility notes where possible.

Community PRs keep the list current as vendors change programs.
